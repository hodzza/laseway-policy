Laseway Hub Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 03.13.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Laseway Hub. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined here.

2. Data We Collect

We collect and store the following user information:

  • Personal Information: First name, last name, date of birth, date of registration.
  • Gym Subscription Data: Number of workouts, membership expiration date.
  • Body Measurements: Waist, hip, thigh, upper arm, chest circumference, height, weight, and progress images.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Manage gym memberships and subscriptions.
  • Track body measurement progress.
  • Provide a personalized fitness experience.
  • Ensure proper functionality of the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored in our remote database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties. Your personal information remains confidential and is used solely for the intended app functionality.

6. Data Deletion Request

Users have the right to request the deletion of their account and associated data. When a deletion request is made, all user data will be permanently removed from our database. To request deletion, please contact us at kontakt@laseway.fit.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at kontakt@laseway.fit.
